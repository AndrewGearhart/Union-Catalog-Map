The Union Catalog Map

Welcome to the Union Catalog on a Map Experiment.

A new experimental interactive map is now available. The map allows you to to view the publication of metamorphic pictures geographically throughout time as well as search for specific items and view their details by clicking on the markers. The map displays the same information maintained in the union catalog. Please note that the map is an experiment and in the very early stages of development.
